Veterans Program

One free exterior job per month for a veteran.

Each month, DSG Exterior Cleaning selects one veteran (or veteran’s household) in our service area for a complimentary exterior cleaning project, based on nominations, safety, and scheduling fit.

Local focus

This program is our way of giving back locally, at a scale we can support consistently. We focus on realistic projects where we can make a clear, visible difference.

  • Open to veterans in Dutchess, Putnam, and Upper Westchester
  • One project scheduled per month (subject to weather/season)
  • Scope determined by safety and practical access
How nominations work
  1. Complete the short nomination form below.
  2. Share a brief story and basic property details.
  3. We review nominations each month.
  4. We contact the selected household to confirm scope and schedule.

Not all nominations can be selected. If your nomination isn’t chosen in a given month, we may keep it on file for future consideration.
