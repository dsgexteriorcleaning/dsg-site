Pricing

Bundles and Home Care subscriptions.

We focus on structured bundles and maintenance plans that keep your exterior looking cared for year-round. Pricing below is designed for professional, insured work — not “quick blasts.” All amounts are typical Dutchess County baselines and are shown before applicable NY sales tax. Putnam and Upper Westchester generally price 10–20% higher based on operating costs.

Subscriptions

DSG Home Care Plans.

Three tiers of annual maintenance that bundle your most important services into predictable, scheduled visits. Built to match how homes in Dutchess, Putnam, and Upper Westchester actually weather over time.

Essential Home Care

Dutchess baseline: $45/mo or $495/yr

  • 1× House Soft Wash (≤ ~2,500 sq ft)
  • 1× Gutter Cleaning
  • Priority scheduling

Optional “Essential Driveway Add-On”: +$20/mo or +$220/yr for one standard driveway or front walkway cleaning per year.

Complete Home Care

Dutchess baseline: $85/mo or $945/yr

  • 1× House Soft Wash (≤ ~3,200 sq ft)
  • 1× Gutter Cleaning
  • 1× Exterior Window Cleaning (standard counts)
  • 1× Driveway or front flatwork cleaning
  • Priority scheduling & 10% off additional services

Ideal for homeowners who want siding, gutters, windows, and a key hardscape surface taken care of each year.

Estate Home Care

Dutchess baseline: $135/mo or $1,495/yr

  • 1× House Soft Wash (≤ ~4,000 sq ft; larger homes quoted individually)
  • 2× Gutter Cleanings per year
  • 1× Exterior Window Cleaning
  • 1× Full driveway + front walkway cleaning
  • Concierge scheduling and planning

Designed for higher-end or larger homes that benefit from more frequent gutter care and full hardscape attention.

Putnam pricing is generally ~10% higher, and Upper Westchester ~20% higher than these Dutchess baselines. Final pricing depends on size, access, and condition. All plan pricing is pre–NY sales tax.
