Services

Exterior cleaning, done carefully.

Methods matched to materials: soft washing where low pressure is required, controlled pressure on concrete and flatwork, and ground-first window and gutter tools wherever possible.

Soft Washing (House Washing)

Low-pressure application of cleaning solutions designed to remove organic growth (mold, mildew, algae) from siding and trim without the damage risk of uncontrolled high pressure.

  • Ideal for vinyl, aluminum, and fiber cement
  • Two-story capability from the ground in many cases
  • Careful plant and property protection
Roof Soft Washing (as applicable)

Gentle soft washing for suitable asphalt shingle roofs, following strict stop-work and safety guidelines. Not every roof is a candidate; we will tell you honestly if it isn’t.

  • Targets black streaks and organic growth
  • Safety and access evaluated before scheduling
  • Equipment and ladder limits respected—no shortcuts
Concrete, Driveways & Flatwork

Surface cleaner and controlled pressure to clean driveways, walkways, and patios. Many of our Home Care Plans include driveway cleaning as part of the annual visit.

  • Driveways, front walks, and select patio areas
  • Configured for your property size and layout
  • Bundled in Curb Appeal and Premium Reset packages
Gutter Cleaning & Brightening

Debris removal to restore flow, using ground tools where possible. Optional gutter brightening addresses certain surface staining on the exterior of the gutter.

  • 1–2 story homes; complex roofs evaluated individually
  • Downspouts checked for basic flow
  • Brightening is a separate, surface treatment service
Window Cleaning

Exterior window cleaning using traditional tools and water-fed poles; interior cleaning available on request.

  • Exterior and optional interior packages
  • Water-fed pole where feasible for safer access
  • Included in Complete and Estate Home Care Plans
Home Care Plans & Bundles

Structured maintenance plans and high-value bundles that keep your home consistently cared for — house, gutters, windows, and driveway — with predictable pricing.

  • Essential, Complete, and Estate Home Care Plans
  • Bundled “Curb Appeal” and “Full Exterior” packages
  • Designed around your season and property type
