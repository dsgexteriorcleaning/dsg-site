About

Exterior cleaning that respects your home.

DSG Exterior Cleaning exists to give Hudson Valley homeowners a premium, care-first option for soft washing, pressure washing, window cleaning, and gutter care — with clear standards, insured technicians, and predictable scheduling.

Care-first by design

Anyone can point a pressure washer at a house. Our focus is using the right methods, chemicals, and equipment for each surface so your siding, roofing, windows, and hardscapes are cleaned without being abused.

  • Soft washing where low pressure is required
  • Controlled pressure on concrete and flatwork only
  • Plant and property protection as part of every job

If something isn’t a good candidate for cleaning, we’ll explain why instead of forcing a result.

Built around the Hudson Valley

We serve Dutchess, Putnam, and Upper Westchester — so our standards, pricing, and scheduling are tuned to local home styles, weather patterns, and expectations.

  • Route-based scheduling for realistic arrival windows
  • Premium pricing aligned with insured, trained labor
  • Seasonal planning that respects your actual use of the property
Clear process, no surprises

We keep things simple and predictable: written quotes, defined scopes of work, and clear communication before, during, and after service.

  • Written estimates before scheduling
  • Realistic weather and safety policies
  • Photos or walkthroughs when appropriate
Long-term mindset

DSG Home Care Plans and curated bundles are designed to maintain your property over time, not just “blast it once and hope for the best.”

  • Annual and multi-service plans
  • Focus on material life and appearance, not just today’s photos
  • Veterans program with one no-charge job each month
