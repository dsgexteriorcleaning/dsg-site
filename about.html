About

Exterior cleaning, done like you’d want it done at your own home.

DSG Exterior Cleaning was created around a simple idea: give Hudson Valley homeowners a premium, care-first option for soft washing, pressure washing, windows, and gutters — with clear standards, insured technicians, and no “rush and blast” mentality.

Care-first by design

Our background is in structured, process-driven environments — where details matter, safety matters, and doing the job correctly matters more than doing it quickly.

  • Soft washing where low pressure is required
  • Ground-based tools and water-fed poles whenever possible
  • Stop-work rules when conditions aren’t safe or appropriate

If something isn’t a good fit for our methods or equipment, we say so — and explain why.

Built for Hudson Valley homeowners

We actively serve Dutchess, Putnam, and Upper Westchester. That means we’re tuned into local building styles, typical staining patterns, seasonal constraints, and realistic expectations for what can and can’t be cleaned.

  • Route-based scheduling to keep windows realistic
  • Pricing aligned with trained, insured labor
  • Clear communication before, during, and after the job
Our standards
  • No high-pressure siding blasting
  • No surprise add-ons at the door
  • Written quotes and clear scopes
  • Respect for landscaping and surroundings
